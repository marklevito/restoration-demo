Straight answers

The questions every owner asks.

Does it sound like a robot? +

No — natural and conversational, and calm with stressed callers. People regularly don't realize they're talking to an AI. If they ask, it's honest about it. Click Talk to the AI above and judge for yourself.

What happens in a real emergency? +

Safety comes first. For active fire, a gas smell, electrical hazards near water, or Category 3 sewage with vulnerable people, it gives the right steps immediately — leave the building, shut the valve, call 911 — then flags the job URGENT and notifies your on-call tech fast by text, email, or call.

Can it handle adjusters, TPAs, and program work? +

Yes. It captures carrier, claim, and assignment numbers accurately, routes program calls to the right person, and follows your rules on which programs you accept — while never confirming a customer or quoting coverage it shouldn't.

Do I have to change my phone number or my calendar? +

No. You keep your existing number, and it books straight into the calendar you already use. Route all calls to it, or just your overflow and after-hours emergency line. Nothing about how you run jobs changes — you just stop missing calls.

Can it reschedule or cancel inspections too? +

Yes — it handles the whole appointment, not just the booking. A customer can call back to move or cancel an inspection, and the AI updates your calendar instantly so it's never double-booked or holding a slot it shouldn't.

How fast can I be live, and am I locked in? +

Most setups are live within days. Plans are month-to-month — no long-term contract, cancel anytime. It should earn your business every month.
